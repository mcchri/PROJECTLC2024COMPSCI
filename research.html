What connection exists between computer science and well-being?


Computer science can be implemented with health and mental-being in many ways for example how Fitbits are used to count the steps or how some apps on our phones increase our health. The health factors include regular exercise, nutritional diet, enough sleep, self-esteem, achieving goals, and mental clarity. I researched different systems that were related to well-being and improving the brain. Apps Lumosity (improves memory by playing games), MindNode (helps to brainstorm), and Cognifit (helps to develop the brain). I chose a system that correlates with Lumosity because it is a game on the LED screen of the micro:bit that helps to improve memorization. I rejected the other systems due to their complexity. I abstracted some of the mental health activities from ither features because those features are alike to my needs in this project.


My chosen system:


My idea is a recognizable game called 'Bop It!', which prioritizes how well you can memorize certain patterns. My idea is much more simple because there are only 4 patterns to memorize. Here are the following patterns.



The game shows one pattern and disappears, the user has to click a button for that pattern and then the number of patterns increases if the player gets the sequence right but if the player gets it wrong they lose the game and restart to one pattern. The code will be written on the micro:bit website and combined with Python to send the information to Firebase. The micro:bit code is delivered to the micro:bit via the cable and the pattern output is shown on the LED screen. There are two LEDs, one green and one red to signify if the player got the sequence correct or wrong. This game is targeted to young people from the age of 12 to the age of 18 because young people nowdays are surrounded by technology that depletes their attention span.

My product will allow me to meet my basic and advanced requirements.
